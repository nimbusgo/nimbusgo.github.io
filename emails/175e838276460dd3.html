Order food and send it as a gift.

^You can do that now. 

Now you can send food to your friends (in different cities too). Here’s how to do it: 

        1. Enter their address 
        2. Add food they’d like 
        3. Select "Send as a gift" at checkout
        4. Add a message/gift card if you want
        5. Place the order! 

Need some ideas? Check out our gifting collection. Also, when you spend $20 or more on a gift order, you'll get a $10 off promo code for your next order within 48hrs.
